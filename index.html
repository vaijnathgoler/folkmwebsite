1958

The Foundation

Walter Williams founded Williams Tax Service in Los Angeles, establishing a practice built on knowledgeable tax advice and ensuring clients comply with all federal, state and local tax laws.

1970s

A Partnership Forms

Walter welcomed business partners Dave Childress and Judge Davis into the firm. Dave brought his tax knowledge and existing clients, while Judge focused on business development and attracting new clients. Together they positioned the company as the top Tax Advisors for many Angelenos.

1986

The Next Generation

Walter passed away and his daughter Jackie took over the daily operations. Later, the sons of both Dave and Judge would join the firm, carrying forward the legacy of knowledgeable, discrete financial service.

2003

Expanding Our Reach

Over two decades, our firm began providing financial services to high net worth individuals including numerous athletes and entertainers. We changed our name to Williams Tax & Financial Group, Inc. to reflect our expanded service offerings.

2020

Folk Williams Today

We rebranded as Folk Williams Financial Management, Inc. and upgraded our services to better serve our diverse clientele. Today, we offer a full range of financial services — Tax Planning and Preparation, Business Management, and Accounting — to clients across the globe.
