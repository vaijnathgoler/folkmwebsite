Entity Architecture

The right structure
protects what you are building.

Choosing between LLC, S-Corp, and C-Corp is not a one-time decision. It is a strategic lever that should be revisited as revenue, risk, and exit plans evolve. We advise on formation, restructuring, and dissolution.

L

LLC

Flexible pass-through taxation, limited liability, and minimal compliance burden for early-stage and solo operators.

S

S-Corporation

Self-employment tax savings, pass-through treatment, and credibility for established service and product businesses.

C

C-Corporation

Ideal for venture-backed startups, QSBS eligibility, and businesses planning to retain earnings or go public.
